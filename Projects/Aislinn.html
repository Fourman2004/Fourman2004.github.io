Aislinn

Commentary:

Aislinn is my third and final university project. after a shaky ending to the last project, I was hesitent to do work on another unreal project in a team. Nonetheless, This was came to be. At the beginning there was three programmers, yet one intermitted. This project was one of my favourites to work on, primarily because I got to try things I wouldn't noramlly do or even knew about. I did have my issues with some of the team members, mainly with getting cut off when trying to explain something. Overall, This was much better than last year.


The game - an elevator pitch:

Aislinn is a 3D Exploration game where you play as Sandman's daughter, Aislinn, going through dreams and defeating nightmares to find your father.
