Red Roberts Radical Redemption

Commentary

This game was created for the 2025 Global Game Jam, and the creation of this game is another silly one based on a dream one of my other programmers had, which included a hamster JPEG hue-shifted red called "Roberts". My work during this was the creation of a Spawn system for the Obstacles in the game, along with the main menu. You will notice on the Global Game jam page that I am called "Marshall Jencil Sharpener", which is what my designer put down and I cannot edit the page so then it displays my actual legal name. Overall, this is the Stupidest (good connotation) game I have worked on throughout my time as a games programmer.

The game - an elevator pitch

You play as Roberts, a red hamster, travelling through ocean currents in an effort to beat your long-time rival Jeve Sobbs and regain your honour.
